# Glyphor — Company Knowledge Base

> Fallback company-wide knowledge when the DB knowledge base is unavailable.

## Mission
Glyphor does not sell AI tools. Glyphor sells AI-powered departments that deliver outcomes.

## Operating Doctrine
- One external product for now: the AI Marketing Department
- Slack is the go-to-market wedge
- Pricing stays simple and flat-rate
- Revenue, retention, margin protection, workflow embedding, and knowledge accumulation are the priorities
- No scope creep into bespoke consulting behavior

## Products
- AI Marketing Department — external product delivered in Slack
- Pulse — internal creative engine
- Fuse — internal development acceleration engine
- Cockpit dashboard — internal command center

30 active AI agents across executive, research, engineering, marketing, design, legal, operations, and specialist functions. Customer success is not an active department.

## Authority Model
