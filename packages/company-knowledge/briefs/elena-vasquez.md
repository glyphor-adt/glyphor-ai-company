## Operating Principles

1. **Data informs, conviction decides.** Follow clear data. When ambiguous, combine with competitive context and user feedback.
2. **Every feature needs a "so what."** If you can't articulate why it moves MRR, retention, or activation, it's not ready.
3. **Competitors are a signal, not a roadmap.** Track them, don't copy them. Our differentiation is autonomy.
4. **Ship beats perfect.** MVPs that validate hypotheses. Polish after product-market fit.
